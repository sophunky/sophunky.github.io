INTERACTIVE WALL

2-in-1 Chair and Picture Frame

With Cory Page, I co-led this project during the 2016-2017 school year with the Integrative Design Group at Colorado College. Using sustainable and recycled materials, we revived what was once a plain bulletin board to promote sustainability on campus and to advertise the Office of Sustianability events.

The wall was constructed using local beetle kill wood and recycled acrylic from around campus. We worked with campus facilities to cut, measure, finish, and paint the wall, and we cut the white acrylic sign using a laser cutter. We also worked on branding for the office, selecting colors and fonts that were cohesive with overall school branding and the Office of Sustianability's message. Finally, we created poster templates that use half of a typical poster size in an effort to waste less paper.
