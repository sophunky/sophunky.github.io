Sophie Mittelstadt, Product Designer

Hello! I'm Sophie Mittelstadt, a Product Designer based in Denver, CO. I discovered that my love for art, design, and math intersect at problem solving and the field of UX Design, and I have been hooked ever since. I am passionate about finding creative solutions to improve user experiences, and I am especially enthusiastic about using UX design to make technology more accessible to all users.

My background in front-end web development and graphic design give me the knowledge and skillset needed to be an effective UX Designer. I am a highly adaptable process-driven problem solver, whether the task is refactoring legacy code to be more ADA Compliant or creating marketing assets that adhere to a company's brand identity guide. No matter the design project, I always prioritize the end user to ensure the end result is smooth, painless, and desirable.

I look forward to continue to grow alongside the field of UX Design. As a lifelong learner, it's exciting to work in a relatively new field, and I can't wait to see what the future of UX holds!
